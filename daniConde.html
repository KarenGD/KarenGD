Context

Daniel Conde is a professional growth coach dedicated to helping people secure their desired jobs by leveraging the advantages of the internet and remote work. In order to establish effective communication with his target audience, the personal branding of Dani Conde was meticulously crafted. This branding endeavor aims to authentically reflect Daniel's values, personality, mission, and vision as both an individual and a coach, ensuring a lasting, positive, and engaging experience for the people it is directed towards.

Role

I spearheaded the entire development of the visual communication strategy, encompassing everything from information gathering, mood board creation, to logo proposals and the development of the identity manual. This comprehensive approach ensured that the brand could be consistently and effectively applied across all communication materials, resonating with Daniel's audience in a meaningful and impactful way.

Objectives

Build a strong and positive reputation through the proper management of personal branding on digital platforms.

Develop an identity manual that provides the necessary parameters for the correct application of branding across different communication channels.

Enhance the project's positioning across all media through a solid, coherent, and effective branding strategy.

start point

To determine Dani Conde's unique value proposition, a comparative analysis was conducted with accounts sharing the niche of remote work, digital nomadism, and professional coaching. The aim was to establish a starting point in the strategy that highlights Dani Conde's qualities compared to competitors and positions him as a reference in the industry.Â  The analysis took on account every visual aspect such as colors, logos, type of content and reach of the competition accounts.

Ideal Buyer Persona Avatar:

Creating an ideal buyer persona avatar is essential for establishing a strong emotional connection with the target audience. As a brand that values empathy and aims to build closer, more personal relationships with users, it's crucial to understand their needs, pain points, and aspirations. By creating an avatar profile, I got a deeper understanding of the audience and tailor the marketing efforts to better meet their needs and expectations.
